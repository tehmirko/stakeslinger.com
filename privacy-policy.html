Privacy Policy / Aviso de Privacidad

Last updated: 2 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Stakeslinger ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our website stakeslinger.com and our mobile applications (the "Apps") distributed via Google Play and the Apple App Store.

Stakeslinger is based in Mexico. This notice is issued in accordance with the Mexican Federal Law on Protection of Personal Data Held by Private Parties (LFPDPPP) and, for users in the European Union/EEA, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Note: This is a general notice covering Stakeslinger and its apps. Where an individual app collects data differently, an app-specific notice is provided inside that app and on its store listing (Google Play "Data safety" and Apple "App Privacy").

1. Data controller (Responsable)

Stakeslinger — Mexico. See our Legal Notice for full contact details. Privacy requests: tehstakeslinger@gmail.com.

2. What data we collect

Depending on the app and how you use it, we may process the following categories of data:

We do not knowingly collect data from children below the age required by applicable law without appropriate consent. We do not collect sensitive personal data, and we do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling.

3. Legal basis

  • Mexico (LFPDPPP, 2025): processing is based on your consent (which, under the 2025 Federal Law, may be tacit unless the law requires express consent) and the purposes set out in this notice; we apply the principles of legality, consent, information, quality, purpose, loyalty, proportionality and accountability.
  • EU/EEA (GDPR): contract (Art. 6(1)(b)), legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)), consent (Art. 6(1)(a)), and legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)).

4. Third-party services

Our apps may use third-party providers that process data on our behalf. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase / Google Cloud — backend, authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting. Our Firebase data is hosted in the europe-west (Vienna / Belgium region, EU).
  • Google Play Services / Apple App Store — distribution, in-app purchases.
  • Add any other SDKs your specific app uses (ads, analytics, maps, etc.)

5. International data transfers

Stakeslinger operates from Mexico, while our backend (Google Firebase / Google Cloud) stores and processes data on servers located in the European Union. As a result, your data may be transferred between Mexico and the EU, and processed by Google in those regions.

For EU/EEA users, such transfers outside the EEA are protected by appropriate safeguards (e.g. the EU Standard Contractual Clauses and Google's data processing terms). For Mexican users, international transfers are carried out in accordance with the LFPDPPP and its Regulations.

6. Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law. You can request deletion at any time (see section 8).

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Your rights

Mexican users (ARCO rights): you may request Access, Rectification, Cancellation, or Objection regarding your personal data, and withdraw consent, by contacting us at the email below. If you consider your rights were not properly addressed, you may contact the Mexican data protection authority, the Secretaría Anticorrupción y Buen Gobierno (SABG), which assumed these functions from the former INAI under the 2025 reform.

EU/EEA users (GDPR): you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object, and to data portability, and may lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

To exercise any right, contact tehstakeslinger@gmail.com.

Account & data deletion: email us with the subject "Data deletion" to request deletion of your account and associated data.

9. Changes

We may update this notice. Changes are posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

10. Contact
